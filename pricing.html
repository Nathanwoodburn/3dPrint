3D Printing Pricing

3D Printing pricing is based on the end product's weight and the chosen material and the printing time.

  • ABS+ $0.15/g + $1/hr (For strong & mechanical parts. Avaliable in black)
  • ABS $0.15/g + $1.50/hr (For strong & mechanical parts. Avaliable in blue)
  • TPU $0.30/g + $1.50 hr (For Flexible parts. Avaliable in black)

Minimal order $5

CAD Pricing

CAD (Computer Aided  Design) pricing is based on the time it takes to design and the complexity of the design

  • Quick and easy designs such as this vacuum nozzle adapter cost approx. $5
  • Slower and more difficult designs such as this Airer Connector cost approx. $15
  • Hard to measure designs such as this gear or head torch mount cost approx. $20

All CAD designs come with .stl files and Fusion 360 Files.
